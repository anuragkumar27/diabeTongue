Tongue Diagnosis & Diabetes

Tongue Diagnosis History

Tongue diagnosis has been a cornerstone of Traditional Chinese Medicine for over 2,000 years, providing insights into internal health conditions.

Diabetes & Your Tongue

Changes in tongue color, coating, and texture can indicate blood sugar imbalances. Diabetes often manifests as a thick coating or distinct tongue cracks.
